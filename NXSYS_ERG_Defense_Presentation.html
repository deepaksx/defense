Section 06 — Philosophy

Delivery Philosophy — Fit-to-Standard First, Greenfield Implementation

Fit-to-Standard Approach

Before any custom development is considered, we demonstrate how S/4HANA and the msg industry solutions handle the requirement out of the box. Custom development is a last resort — not a default. Every RICEFW object requires a formal business justification, an NXSYS effort estimate, ERG sign-off, and Steering Committee approval.

Hard Cap: 225 RICEFW Objects
Ceiling, not target. Protects timeline, budget, supportability, and future S/4HANA upgradability.
Why Greenfield — 5 Reasons
1
Wrong architecture from Day 1. PP Discrete cannot be converted to PP-PI. The production module must be replaced.
2
Fragmented master data. Inconsistent codes across entities require a clean data model designed from scratch.
3
Legacy customisations are liabilities. ZROH material types, custom gate passes — accumulated technical debt.
4
msg solutions deliver maximum value on a clean system. Dairy by msg and Meat & Fish by msg are greenfield-native.
5
Company code structure has no design rationale. 2xxx, 1000, 7xxx, 5000 — this must be redesigned.
